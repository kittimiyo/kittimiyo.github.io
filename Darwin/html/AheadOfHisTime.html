AHEAD OF HIS TIME
 
         

 

 

There where many famous people in history who were not liked or believed in their own time. Charles Darwin is a wonderful example. He was afraid at first to come out and tell the world his ideas. It eventually took a young scientist named Alfred Wallace who was steadily catching up on his theories.( Darwin did come up with them first). That finally convinced Darwin to go public, and even then his theories aren't taken seriously. Some people just wouldn't believe him, some people bend his ideas to make it some way to advertise for racism (they say that some people are less evolved, such as poor people). All in all, it was very hard for Darwin.

Charles Darwin must have been thinking ahead of his time, to come up with an idea so new. But it wasn't as new as many people think. The idea of evolution had been around for a very long time. Darwin had just thought of a theory on how it occurred, which is still quite a lot.
